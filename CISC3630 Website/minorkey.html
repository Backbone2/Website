Make sure you're writing down your info!

You can always check out the Chord Reference to find out exactly what notes you need to play. There is absolutely no shame in writing down every single note until you get accustomed to your instrument. I promise, the more you do it, the easier it gets!

Working with a Minor Key?

So you've chosen Minor! Just find your Key in the far left column and look straight across. Those are the chords that will work for the Key you've selected. Feel free to try any chord in the row to try to mix up your progression. The only rule is to make it sound good!
